# TailorTrack Product Plan

## First Version

1. Admin dashboard with daily totals and pending work.
2. Inventory for readymade clothes and unstitched fabric.
3. Customer profiles with measurements and style notes.
4. Sales flow for readymade, unstitched, and stitched garments.
5. Stitching order workflow with tailor assignment and statuses.
6. Tailor rates and salary calculation from completed orders.
7. Payment tracking and unpaid/partial/paid status.

## Later AI Ideas

- Clean rough order notes into structured stitching instructions.
- Summarize customer preferences from past orders.
- Suggest tailor assignment based on workload.
- Flag delivery delay risk.
- Generate customer WhatsApp/SMS pickup messages.
